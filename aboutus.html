Our Mission

Americas Threat Prevention (ATP) is a privately-owned security group that is contracted by businesses & organizations to do an assortment of jobs (site protection, etc). ATP will not conduct itself in foreign matters, and will only commence operations within Americas borders. American Threat Protection specializies in protection of property of assets using advanced weaponary to prevent any possible threats.

With the objective of suppressing and effectively putting an end to gun-related crimes, ATP has successfully sold over 600 million worth of products nationwide to an assortment of facilities; ensuring safety since 1967. Our protected facilities have rid any sort of related gun violences.

The Competition

ATP is unrivaled in the field of autonomous and quick site protection. Our products are technologically advanced, offering the best in almost all aspects- protection, reliability, quickness. Some of our competitors include GENESIS Security and Facility Protection Services.

GENESIS Security

GENESIS makes up for its lack of technology and effective site protection (and security) in publicity and fame. GENESIS Security utilizes human security guards to do jobs that don’t allow for error, making the quality of work reduced & rushed. The average delay time (on average) is usually upwards of 20 minutes, meaning the damage could’ve already been done. GENESIS also doesn’t offer benefits, and support is inconsistent.

Facility Protection Services

Although recognized as one of the more competent forces, FPS (facility protection services) lacks presence and resources. Despite their skilled & talented members, the average time of arrival is roughly 15~ minutes, meaning prevention isn’t an option. FPS relies primarily on humans, with specialized mechanical machines that can perform specific jobs, making them susceptible to human error.
