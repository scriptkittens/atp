Common Inquiries

Over the past decade, ATP has received a lot attention- especially in its site security. The following pages/steps will be dedicated as common inquiries & more information into our products and their capabilities.

Question Number #1 | 3/1/2023

How do I purchase one of your products?

To purchase one of our products, refer to our "About Me" page and contact us via our email. Payment information & site location will be discussed there in a private ticket.

Question Number #2 | 3/25/2023

If I do purchase one of your products, how would I get the cameras installed? Would I be able to recieve continued support?

If you purchase a ATP product, all information relating to said project would be discussed in a ticket after purchase (such as site location, details, exceptions, etc). We have 24/7 support; and all prior-customers of ATP have the ability to access it (technical issues, questions, etc). Please refer to our "About Us" page to figure out more.

Question Number #3 | 3/18/2023

How does the machiney work?

All the technical information about our products can be found in the "Plan" section.

Question Number #4 | 3/29/2023

I operate a facility that includes private personnel armed. How do I inform the device to not target friendlies?

Some of our products (if capable) ship with a requested amount of chips that relay signals to the main hub that identify it as a friendly. Simply ask your handeler for a specific amount and a unique chip designed for your device only will be shipped out to the set-up location.
